What is a dog?

A dog is a type of domesticated animal. Known for its loyalty and faithfulness, it can be found as a welcome guest in many households across the world.

What kinds of dogs are there?

There are many breeds of dogs. Each breed varies in size and temperament. Owners often select a breed of dog that they find to be compatible with their own lifestyle and desires from a companion.

How do you acquire a dog?

Three common ways for a prospective owner to acquire a dog is from pet shops, private owners, or shelters.

A pet shop may be the most convenient way to buy a dog. Buying a dog from a private owner allows you to assess the pedigree and upbringing of your dog before choosing to take it home. Lastly, finding your dog from a shelter, helps give a good home to a dog who may not find one so readily.

Matching mentors to mentees

We typically look to match mentees to mentors with common interests or backgrounds. This can include any of the following – secondary school, university, degree, ethnic background, regional background, personal or business interests. Alternatively, if you feel that there is a certain personality type that you would be best suited to mentoring we can also look to ensure your mentee fits this profile.

SEO Scholars

Mentoring on the Scholars programme requires less than one hour a week of your time with the majority of interaction taking place over a secure online e-mentoring platform. This is extremely easy to use and will make interacting with your mentee simple and convenient wherever you are.

As the beneficiaries of the Scholars programme are 18 and under, there is a level of safeguarding that we must adhere to. All volunteers offering to become mentors for the Scholars programme must complete a CRB application for an Enhanced Disclosure and attend a child protection & mentoring training session. If you are interested in becoming a mentor contact Namrata Shanbhag at Namrata.Shanbhag@seo-london.org/ scholars@seo-london.org

SEO Careers

We want our interns to have the best possible experience during their internships so they will be the top hiring choice at their firm at the end of the summer. Students are allocated an industry mentor to help guide them through their summer experience and ensure they outperform their peers.

We would hope that the time commitment should be no more than a few hours over the course of the summer. This would include meeting the student at least once for a coffee or lunch and then being available for follow up calls and emails during their summer placements. If a student requires any in-depth support this is something that will be provided by the SEO staff and alumni network, where possible.

If you are interested in becoming a mentor please contact Chioma Oganya at Chioma.Oganya@seo-london.org/ careers@seo-london.org

Become a Sponsor Firm

SEO can help corporate sponsors access the brightest and most talented students from under-represented and under-served communities at both secondary school (SEO Scholars) and university (SEO Careers) level for mentoring, internship and graduate recruitment opportunities.

benefits for Sponsors:

  • A national pipeline to the UK’s most talented secondary school and university students from under-represented communities.  SEO partners with leading non fee paying schools across all 32 London Education Authorities and visits 50+ UK university campuses every year.
  • SEO’s extensive screening, interviewing and selection process to choose the strongest candidates for SEO partners. By working closely with its partners, SEO gains a comprehensive understanding of the requirements for success in each environment.
  • Opportunity to host an executive roundtable, seminar, or reception to showcase your organisation's mission, culture, and opportunities to the broader candidate group.
  • SEO partners with industry leading firms across a huge range of career paths.  This includes global companies, prestigious investment banks and technology firms, top-ranked corporate law firms, and elite strategy consultancy firms and advertising and marketing agencies.
